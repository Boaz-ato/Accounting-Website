Our expertise in taxation has benefitted many of our clients. We’re dedicated to our clients and help them save taxes through proper and well informed tax advice. We develop our tax knowledge through regular tax training courses for our staff. This keeps us current on tax law so we’re always ready for the toughest situations. We have a high satisfaction rating from our clients when it comes to accounting & tax matters and have provided excellent tax planning advice to our clients.



At Hackman & CO Chartered Certified Accountants we use HM Revenue & Customs-approved software to :

  • Complete your tax return

  • Calculate your tax liability

  • File the return online

  • Liaise with you on the amounts to be paid and when they are due

As part of the above service, we can analyse your self assessment tax return to see if any tax savings can be made and we can also review the form to see if there are any anomalies that need to be addressed before the return is submitted.

By giving you a fixed, competitive price, we can take the worry away when it comes to self assessment tax returns; allowing you to concentrate on running your business.
