Hackman & CO Chartered Certified Accountants accountants provide accurate and comprehensive bookkeeping services to suit your needs. Everything you would expect from a professional bookkeeper in Barking and Dagenham.

We provide accurate and comprehensive bookkeeping service to suit your needs

It may feel like you can save money by doing everything in your business yourself, however using a local bookkeeper can actually save you more money and time in the long run. These savings come from a reduced level of risk of human error, years of experience working with many other clients, no missed payments to HMRC or missed tax deadlines. Add to this the benefits of tax planning and business reporting you'll see that choosing a local bookkeeper is one of the best decisions you can make.



At Hackman & CO Chartered Certified Accountants, we provide you with a range of bookkeeping services which include using the latest cloud software and apps, removing manual record keeping tasks and improving your overall efficiency.

You can benefit from:

  • Real-time, valuable insight into your business

  • More control over your numbers

  • Better control over your numbers

  • Greater foresight to grow your business

  • Time back for you either in the business or at home

We will then record all of your business’s transactions and give you 24/7 access. We can also prepare regular management accounts to help you manage your finances, your customer and supplier account balances, stock levels and if you are trading as a company, we will allow you to plan your dividends.
